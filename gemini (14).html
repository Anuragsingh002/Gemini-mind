Our Technology

Multi-Modal Sensor Fusion

We combine electrical and hemodynamic monitoring to create the world's most robust cognitive model.

EEG (Electrical)

Measures voltage fluctuations resulting from ionic current within the neurons of the brain.

  • Temporal Resolution: ~1ms
  • Detects: Focus, Alertness

fNIRS (Hemodynamic)

Uses near-infrared light to measure blood oxygenation levels in the prefrontal cortex.

  • Spatial Resolution: High
  • Detects: Workload, Fatigue

Behavioral AI

Correlates biological signals with keystroke dynamics, mouse movement, and task performance.

  • Contextual Awareness
  • Detects: Stress, Hesitation
