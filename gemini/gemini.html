A Multi-Layered Approach to Cognition

EEG Analysis

High-resolution EEG captures brainwave patterns that reveal attention span, cognitive workload, and mental fatigue.

fNIRS Imaging

fNIRS tracks blood oxygenation in the prefrontal cortex to decode problem-solving depth and decision stress response.

Keystroke Dynamics

By analyzing typing rhythm and patterns, we identify cognitive fluency, mental fatigue, and adaptability under pressure.

Cognitive Games

Gamified neuro-assessments benchmark attention, memory, and decision-making speed in a controlled environment.

From Brainwaves to Breakthroughs

1. Multimodal Cognitive Signal Capture

Mindxcope's lightweight, non-invasive BCI headset seamlessly collects EEG signals, typing patterns, and physiological data during real-world tasks or cognitive games.

2. AI-Driven Cognitive Intelligence

Our machine learning models decode billions of data points into actionable insights — identifying traits like focus, creativity, resilience, and stress response.

3. Predictive Hiring Dashboard

Mindxcope’s interactive dashboard enables recruiters to compare candidates beyond resumes, using science-backed, bias-free cognitive metrics.

4. Real-Time Mental Fatigue Monitoring

We continuously track cognitive load and fatigue signals — allowing employers to detect burnout risk, monitor attention span, and ensure peak performance zones in both hiring and workforce development.
